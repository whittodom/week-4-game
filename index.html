Crystal Collector: Infinity Stones

Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of each round.

There are four Infinity Stones below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. If your total score goes above the random number, you lose.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

The points alloted to each stone are randomly chosen at the beginning of each round.
